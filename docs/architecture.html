Scoring and clearance

  1. Analyst (Sonnet, Grok fallback) fills per-axis scores; project axes are clamped to evidence-strength bands (emerging / solid / exceptional) and out-of-band scores are rejected into a repair pass.
  2. Engine sums axes per role, applies hard caps (rug / fraud / hard graph link cap to 10, forcing AVOID); the most-severe role governs and ties break to the LOWER score. PASS 70-100.
  3. Clearance needs every never-waive screen recorded (identity, OFAC name + address, trust-graph, founder-asset) plus 75% recorded coverage; else the verdict publishes PROVISIONAL or INCOMPLETE, never silently cleared.

Invariants a change must never break

  • H2: score floors come only from strictly verified facts; corroboration, press, and self-attestation widen ceilings, never mint minimums.
  • Never-waive screens must be recorded for clearance; the trust-graph screen owns a reserved budget window.
  • resolved_name is never inferred; legal and sanctions screens require a provider-resolved real person and always run live (never cached in the KB).
  • Model leads never route roles, cap scores, or floor scores.
  • Immutability: report versions are trigger-protected; every audit runs in an isolated cost ledger and cannot persist without one.
  • Copy: no em or en dashes anywhere in authored copy (CI-enforced).
